PRODUCT DISTRIBUTION AGREEMENT

PARTIES:
- Supplier: Premier Electronics Inc. ("Supplier")
- Distributor: National Tech Distributors LLC ("Distributor")

EFFECTIVE DATE: March 1, 2024
TERM: 24 months

1. APPOINTMENT
Supplier appoints Distributor as a non-exclusive distributor for Supplier's 
consumer electronics products ("Products") in the United States.

2. ORDERS AND PRICING
- Minimum annual purchase commitment: $2,000,000
- Pricing: Per attached Price List (Exhibit A)
- Volume discounts: 5% for orders exceeding $100,000

3. PAYMENT TERMS
- Payment: Net 45 days from invoice
- Credit limit: $500,000

4. RETURNS AND EXCHANGES

*** UNCONDITIONAL RIGHT OF RETURN ***

Distributor shall have the unconditional right to return any unsold Products 
for full credit under the following terms:

a) Return Period: Products may be returned within NINETY (90) DAYS of delivery
b) Condition: Products must be in original, unopened packaging
c) Credit: Full purchase price credit, no restocking fee
d) Shipping: Supplier pays return shipping costs
e) No Questions Asked: Returns accepted for any reason including:
   - Slow-moving inventory
   - Changed market conditions  
   - Customer preference
   - Overstocking

Supplier agrees to process all return credits within 15 business days of 
receiving returned Products.

*** END RETURN POLICY ***

5. INVENTORY PROTECTION
In the event of Product discontinuation, Supplier will repurchase remaining 
inventory at Distributor's cost.

6. MARKETING
Supplier will provide co-op marketing funds equal to 2% of annual purchases.

7. WARRANTY
Supplier provides standard 1-year manufacturer warranty on all Products.
Distributor may offer extended warranty programs at its discretion.

8. TERMINATION
- Either party: 90 days written notice
- Immediate termination for material breach
- Upon termination, all return rights remain valid for 90 days

9. INDEMNIFICATION
Supplier indemnifies Distributor against product liability claims.
