MANAGED IT SERVICES AGREEMENT

PARTIES:
- Provider: SecureTech Managed Services ("Provider")
- Customer: Regional Healthcare Network ("Customer")

EFFECTIVE DATE: May 1, 2024
INITIAL TERM: 36 months

1. SERVICES
Provider shall provide comprehensive managed IT services including:
- 24/7 network monitoring
- Help desk support
- Security management
- Backup and disaster recovery
- Cloud infrastructure management

2. SERVICE LEVELS
- Network uptime: 99.95%
- Help desk response: 15 minutes for critical issues
- Security incident response: Immediate

3. FEES
- Monthly service fee: $85,000
- Annual contract value: $1,020,000
- Total initial term value: $3,060,000

4. PAYMENT
- Payment terms: Net 30 days
- Monthly invoicing in arrears

5. TERM AND RENEWAL

*** AUTOMATIC RENEWAL WITH ESCALATION ***

a) INITIAL TERM: This Agreement shall have an initial term of thirty-six (36) 
   months commencing on the Effective Date.

b) AUTOMATIC RENEWAL: Upon expiration of the Initial Term, this Agreement shall 
   AUTOMATICALLY RENEW for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either 
   party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ONE HUNDRED EIGHTY (180) 
   DAYS prior to the end of the then-current term.

c) PRICE ESCALATION: Upon each automatic renewal, the Monthly Service Fee shall 
   automatically increase by FIVE PERCENT (5%) over the prior term's rate:
   
   - Year 4: $89,250/month ($1,071,000 annual)
   - Year 5: $93,713/month ($1,124,550 annual)
   - Year 6: $98,398/month ($1,180,778 annual)
   - And so forth...

d) NO PRICE CAP: The 5% annual escalation applies to each renewal period with 
   no maximum cap on cumulative increases.

e) BINDING RENEWAL: Failure to provide timely notice of non-renewal shall 
   constitute acceptance of the renewal term at the escalated rate.

*** END RENEWAL TERMS ***

6. TERMINATION
- Termination for cause: 30 days written notice with opportunity to cure
- Early termination fee: 50% of remaining contract value
- No termination for convenience during initial term

7. DATA TRANSITION
Upon termination, Provider shall assist with data migration for up to 90 days 
at then-current hourly rates.

8. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Provider's liability limited to 12 months of service fees.

9. COMPLIANCE
Provider maintains HIPAA compliance certification for healthcare customers.

10. INSURANCE
Provider maintains cyber liability insurance of $5,000,000 per occurrence.
