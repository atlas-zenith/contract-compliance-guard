STRATEGIC SUPPLY AGREEMENT

PARTIES:
- Manufacturer: Industrial Components Corp. ("Manufacturer")
- Buyer: AutoMakers International Inc. ("Buyer")

EFFECTIVE DATE: April 1, 2024
TERM: 60 months (5 years)

1. SUPPLY COMMITMENT
Manufacturer agrees to supply Buyer with precision automotive components 
("Components") as specified in Exhibit A for the term of this Agreement.

2. VOLUME AND PRICING
- Estimated annual volume: 500,000 units
- Base unit price: $45.00 per unit
- Total estimated annual value: $22,500,000

3. PRICE ADJUSTMENTS
Annual price adjustments permitted based on documented raw material cost changes, 
not to exceed 5% per year.

4. PRICE PROTECTION AND MOST FAVORED CUSTOMER CLAUSE

*** MOST FAVORED CUSTOMER GUARANTEE ***

Manufacturer warrants and guarantees that Buyer shall receive the most favorable 
pricing offered to any customer for comparable Components:

a) PRICE MATCHING: If Manufacturer offers a lower price to any other customer 
   for the same or substantially similar Components, Manufacturer shall 
   immediately reduce Buyer's price to match.

b) RETROACTIVE ADJUSTMENT: Price reductions shall be applied retroactively to 
   all purchases made in the current calendar quarter. Manufacturer shall issue 
   credits within 30 days.

c) AUDIT RIGHTS: Buyer may audit Manufacturer's pricing records annually to 
   verify compliance with this clause.

d) MOST FAVORED STATUS: This guarantee applies to:
   - Published list prices
   - Negotiated contract prices
   - Volume discount arrangements
   - Promotional pricing
   - Any other pricing arrangement

e) REBATE GUARANTEE: Any rebates, incentives, or other price concessions offered 
   to other customers shall be offered to Buyer on equivalent terms.

*** END MFC CLAUSE ***

5. QUALITY REQUIREMENTS
- Defect rate: Not to exceed 0.1%
- ISO 9001 certification required
- Right to inspect manufacturing facility

6. DELIVERY
- Delivery terms: FOB Buyer's facility
- Lead time: 4 weeks from purchase order
- Late delivery penalty: 1% per week, capped at 10%

7. INVENTORY
Manufacturer shall maintain 8 weeks of safety stock for Buyer's requirements.

8. EXCLUSIVITY
Manufacturer shall not supply Components to Buyer's direct competitors 
without Buyer's written consent.

9. TERMINATION
- Termination for convenience: 180 days notice
- Termination for cause: 30 days to cure

10. INDEMNIFICATION
Manufacturer indemnifies Buyer against third-party IP claims related to Components.
