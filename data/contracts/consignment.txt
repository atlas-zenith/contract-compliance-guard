CONSIGNMENT AND DISTRIBUTION AGREEMENT

PARTIES:
- Consignor: LuxuryGoods Manufacturing Ltd. ("Consignor")
- Consignee: Elite Retail Partners Inc. ("Consignee")

EFFECTIVE DATE: June 1, 2024
TERM: 24 months

1. CONSIGNMENT ARRANGEMENT
Consignor shall deliver luxury goods ("Products") to Consignee's retail locations 
on a consignment basis for sale to end consumers.

2. PRODUCTS AND LOCATIONS
- Product lines: Premium watches, jewelry, and accessories
- Initial inventory value: $2,500,000
- Locations: 15 Elite Retail stores (Exhibit A)

*** CONSIGNMENT TERMS - TITLE AND RISK ***

3. OWNERSHIP AND CONTROL

a) TITLE RETENTION: Consignor retains full legal title to all consigned Products 
   until sale to an end consumer. Products remain the property of Consignor while 
   in Consignee's possession.

b) RISK OF LOSS: Risk of loss or damage to Products transfers to Consignee upon 
   delivery to Consignee's location, HOWEVER title does NOT transfer.

c) NO PAYMENT UNTIL SALE: Consignee shall have no obligation to pay for Products 
   until such Products are sold to an end consumer. 

d) SALE REPORTING: Consignee shall report all sales weekly and remit payment 
   for sold Products within 30 days of each weekly report.

e) INVENTORY CONTROL: Consignor may inspect inventory at any time with 24-hour 
   notice. Consignee shall maintain accurate inventory records.

f) UNSOLD INVENTORY: Consignor may recall unsold inventory at any time. 
   Consignee shall return recalled Products within 10 business days at 
   Consignor's expense.

g) NO MINIMUM PURCHASE: Consignee has no obligation to purchase any minimum 
   quantity of Products.

*** END CONSIGNMENT TERMS ***

4. PRICING AND COMMISSION
- Retail prices set by Consignor
- Consignee commission: 35% of retail price
- Consignor receives: 65% of retail price

5. DISPLAY AND MARKETING
- Consignee shall display Products in premium locations
- Consignee shall maintain trained sales staff
- Co-op marketing: 3% of sales

6. INSURANCE
- Consignee shall insure Products for full retail value
- Consignor named as loss payee

7. DAMAGE AND LOSS
- Consignee liable for damaged or lost Products at wholesale cost
- Monthly inventory reconciliation required

8. EXCLUSIVITY
Products may only be sold through authorized Elite Retail locations.

9. TERMINATION
- Either party: 60 days written notice
- Upon termination: All unsold Products returned within 30 days
- Consignor responsible for return shipping

10. AUDIT RIGHTS
Consignor may audit Consignee's sales records and inventory annually.
