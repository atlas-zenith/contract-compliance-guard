CUSTOM SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT AGREEMENT

PARTIES:
- Developer: AgileCode Technologies Inc. ("Developer")
- Client: Healthcare Innovations Group ("Client")

EFFECTIVE DATE: January 15, 2024
PROJECT COMPLETION TARGET: September 30, 2024

1. PROJECT SCOPE
Developer shall design, develop, and deploy a custom Patient Management System 
("System") as specified in the Statement of Work (Exhibit A).

2. PROJECT PHASES AND DELIVERABLES
Phase 1: Requirements & Design (8 weeks)
Phase 2: Core Development (12 weeks)
Phase 3: Integration & Testing (8 weeks)
Phase 4: Deployment & Training (4 weeks)

3. FEES AND MILESTONE PAYMENTS

*** MILESTONE-BASED PAYMENT SCHEDULE ***

Total Project Fee: $800,000

Payments are contingent upon successful completion and Client acceptance of 
each milestone:

Milestone 1 - Project Kickoff
- Amount: $80,000 (10%)
- Due: Upon contract execution
- Deliverable: Project charter and team assignment

Milestone 2 - Requirements Sign-Off
- Amount: $120,000 (15%)
- Due: Upon Client approval of requirements document
- Contingency: Client must approve within 10 business days of delivery

Milestone 3 - Design Approval
- Amount: $120,000 (15%)
- Due: Upon Client approval of system design
- Contingency: Approval based on design review meeting

Milestone 4 - Alpha Release
- Amount: $160,000 (20%)
- Due: Upon successful demonstration of core functionality
- Contingency: Must pass 80% of defined test cases

Milestone 5 - Beta Release
- Amount: $160,000 (20%)
- Due: Upon completion of UAT environment
- Contingency: No critical defects outstanding

Milestone 6 - Go-Live & Acceptance
- Amount: $160,000 (20%)
- Due: 30 days after successful production deployment
- Contingency: System must achieve 99% uptime for 30 days

*** PAYMENT RELEASE SUBJECT TO CLIENT WRITTEN ACCEPTANCE ***

All milestone payments require formal written acceptance from Client's Project 
Sponsor. Developer shall not invoice until acceptance is received.

4. CHANGE ORDERS
Additional work outside scope requires written change order with agreed pricing.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Upon final payment, Client shall own all custom code developed specifically 
for this project. Developer retains rights to pre-existing tools and libraries.

6. WARRANTIES
Developer warrants the System will perform per specifications for 12 months.

7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Developer's liability limited to fees actually paid under this Agreement.

8. TERMINATION
Client may terminate for convenience with 30 days notice. 
Developer entitled to payment for accepted milestones plus work in progress.
